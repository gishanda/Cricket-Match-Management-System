Information

Best Sport House is a major Sri Lankan sporting goods chain store, with stores located throughout Sri Lanka. It started with a small shop near Dehiwala, Sri Lanka in 2005. First the business showed some falls. Mr. John(founder of Best Sport House) had a dream of making his company a well known sporting shop in Sri Lanka. His dream came true in 2008 and Best Sport House was on top of the business. The small shop that began this company is now the main branch and is a three storey building full of sport clothing, accessories and equipments.

In 2009 the company entered the American market by opening a big Best Sport House superstore in America(Massachusetts). It showed great improvement in business and Mr.john gained a big profit. In 2010 due to some personal reasons Mr. john announced his decision to exit the US Market by the year end. The retailer stocks a wide range of sporting goods, from tennis racquets to advance scuba diving equipments. Usually Best House Sports is specialized in cricket clothing.
